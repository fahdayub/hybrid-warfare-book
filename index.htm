About the Book

This book was transformed from a doctoral thesis and effectively communicates Pakistan's security perspective.

The book comprises authentic research work reinforced by a comprehensive bibliography citing around 100 primary sources and 515 secondary sources.

The reviewers include Pakistan's former Chairman Joint Chiefs of Staff Committee General Zubair Mahmood Hayat, AJ&K's former President Ambassador Sardar Masood Khan, Pakistan's former Foreign Secretary Ambassador Sohail Mahmood and Dr Asma Shakir Khawaja former Dean National Defence University.

The book examines India's hybrid warfare strategy and its implications for Pakistan's national security across diplomatic, informational, military, economic, financial, intelligence and legal domains.
